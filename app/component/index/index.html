About HacashDAO

THacashDAO is comprised of individuals with a profound comprehension of the fundamental nature of money, acknowledging that Bitcoin might not represent the most ideal currency.We hold a strong conviction that Hacash is poised to become the future's predominant mainstream currency.

Our dedication lies in rectifying the sluggish progress caused by the absence of an official team and the equitable distribution of Hacash.
